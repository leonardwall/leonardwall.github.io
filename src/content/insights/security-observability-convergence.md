Modern enterprises can no longer afford to treat observability and security as separate conversations. The same telemetry that helps engineering teams restore service health can help security teams detect drift, investigate anomalies, and understand blast radius faster.

The opportunity is not simply tool consolidation. It is decision acceleration.

Leaders who treat telemetry as a strategic asset can reduce operational friction, improve resilience, and create a more coherent platform story across operations, engineering, and cyber.
