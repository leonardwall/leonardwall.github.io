About

A platform view shaped by security, operations, and 20 years of leadership

Leonard Wall is a Field CTO at Cisco and a technology executive focused on the convergence of security, observability, and modern platform strategy. His work centers on helping enterprise organizations reduce complexity, improve resilience, and create stronger alignment across engineering, operations, and cyber functions.

His background spans enterprise security leadership, digital transformation, regulatory environments, and executive advisory work across multiple industries. As a former Deputy CISO, he brings a rare combination of board-level perspective and hands-on technical depth — a range that allows him to bridge the strategic and operational layers of modern technology decisions.

Today, his writing and projects focus on platformization, telemetry as a strategic asset, executive decision velocity, and the practical overlap between observability and security — the next frontier for enterprise resilience.
